Welcome to Tweety.PY, your twitter bot!

Features:

  • Make comments with custom multiple scripts on targetted tweets for better engagement!
  • Follow users and comment on their profile follow for follow back to gain followers![upcoming]
  • Gain likes on a post by sharing it on twitter![upcoming]
  • Integrated hashtag library to boost performance of post![upcoming]

How to Use?

First do a post request at /authtoken along with data : email: your email id | cons_key: your twitter user connection key |cons_sec: your twitter user connection secret | access_key:your twitter access key | access_secret: your twitter access key secret||
On success we will send you your email with your api key. This key will be used to authenticate with our servers and use the bot.
Further , you will be given a quota of 100 total executions and a cooldown period of 1 day.

To use the bot :

make a post request at /commentbot with the following data :
  • token: your access token
  • scripts: a list of scripts , will contain strings of sentences. The bot will randomly comment them.
  • tags:list of tags can be hashtags, can be $tags, can be words.
  • nums:int value of number of tweets to comment on.
